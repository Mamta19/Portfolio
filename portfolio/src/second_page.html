Hi, Again! Here you will find my interests, hobbies, passions and professions. Let's begin with the most interesting matter -- my interests. Click on the link and you will find what gets me excited and brightens my day each day everyday.
Do I love to plant trees and care for them? I cannot wait to answer yes to this question. It's no bragging but I have been told by so many that I am born with green fingers. I guess it's the time and energy I put into my yard that makes it so lush green that it feels the grass is telling me "we want to be here." I do believe that plants do understand our emotions and feel our love just like our babies and our pets even though they can't tell us that. Little wonder Spring is my favorite season.

    Winter forces me to be indoor most of the time. This leads me to my another passion: cooking. I have learned many recipes and take ultimate pleasure in cooking for my family and friends. My husband and my boys find so much joy during winter nibbling on their favorite Indian snacks.
